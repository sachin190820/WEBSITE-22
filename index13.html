manifesto
why are we here? and why web3?

Access to the social and financial tools of web3 could usher in a new era of economic and artistic sovereignty. Crypto networks create the potential for individuals to take back power over their currencies and financial systems, and take back control of their data and creativity.

The platforms of the web2 era control much of the data and creative output on the internet. They amassed control and locked their APIs, shutting down countless entrepreneurs and severely constraining the collective potential of the open web. Financial tools have been similarly restricted, with full access limited to the wealthy elite of a select few nation states. Blockchain technology renews the possibility of an open internet and an open financial system.

In order to achieve this potential, we need more beautiful, accessible design, and more compelling and engaging narratives. Current web3 applications are often difficult to use, confusing, and plagued with spam and scams. Public perception of the industry has been stained by these problems, clouding the collective vision of its power and potential.

Over the next five to ten years we anticipate an explosion of new creative solutions to these problems. Not all potential reaches fruition, and we are committed to guiding the industry towards its full realization. We work with teams who share our values and vision and are building at the forefront of this wave of innovation.

our approach

We are future forward & progressive. We love to create bold, dynamic visual design and we are not afraid to experiment and push the envelope. We help teams define and communicate their vision of the future, with full awareness that our collective capacity for imagination is what shapes the trajectory of our technology. We are culturally & philosophically inclined: we bring artistic and contextual awareness to every project we contribute to.

our values

culturally inclined

We aim to cross-pollinate technology and culture. We support and educate creative teams with our deep understanding of web3 technology and we guide technical teams to foster creativity, art and culture within their organizations and communities.

cocreative & collaborative

We are long term collaborators. We become an extension of the teams we partner with and play long term, positive sum games that benefit the entire ecosystem. This approach creates the optimal environment for creative collaboration and maximal innovation.

open & expansive

We believe in open source tools designed with composability and interoperability in mind. No one team or organization should amass total power over the systems we rely on for our economic freedom and access to information.
